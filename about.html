ABOUT US

ESTABLISHED IN 2006

Al Kays International LLC has been established since 2006 with the sole purpose of bringing to Oman a strong knowledge base that offer solutions to its customers in Verticals namely Education, Hospitality and Retail with IT Infrastructure Solutions to compliment our Business.

Kays IT is a dynamic IT company with a diverse portfolio of Products and Solutions in areas of Enterprise Business Applications (Education, Hospitality and Retail) . With a strong engineering background and a vision to help companies excel in their business, we carefully design our solutions to help our customers by suggesting the right technology suited for their environment. With over 2000+ customers in Oman, we are one of the leading IT Companies in the region. With current market trends, we ensure our associations with the right Partners that carry a basket of products and Solutions with the right pricing. As an organization serving customers over the past 10 years, we have a clear road map and have made substantial investments in the region. We have structured training programs for our engineers to ensure that the best in house post sales services can be rendered at the right time. Being a customer-centric organization, our market reach is high and we carry a strong presence in all areas of diverse verticals of Hospitality, Education, Retail, Oil and Gas, Power, Ministries, Manufacturing. Based on the nature of our diverse portfolio, we cater our solutions from SME to large organizations.
